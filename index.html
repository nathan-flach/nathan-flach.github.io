Projects

I’ve led numerous design projects that have focused on solving real world problems across varying disciplines.

Open Design Challenge

To solve the RSA Student Design Awards competition of student financial illiteracy, I designed a multi-faceted solution to solve the question “how might we empower high school students in Ontario to develop the lifelong habits and skills that lead to financial stability?”

To answer this question, I created an engaging financial curriculum, developed a supplementary education model through online learning, and designed a complementary app to manage students’ finances

University of Waterloo Space Design

I found an underused space on campus at the University of Waterloo and after consulting the necessary users and stakeholders, transformed it into an indoor bicycle storage and workshop space using prototyping, scale models, and online sketching tools.

Systems Analyzing

I coordinated a collaborative research paper that applied systems thinking to design a solution that would have improved the rescue effort of the lives and homes of Canadians during the Fort McMurray fire disaster.
